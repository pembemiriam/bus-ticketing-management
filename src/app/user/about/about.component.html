AbOUT US

Crimson Bus Reservation System is a web based system which will help both bus owners and passengers to ease the ticketing process in Buea and in Cameroon in general.This system is build by level 500 students (both Software and Networking) from the prestigious Faculty of Engineering and Technology (FET) in the University of Buea. This system will become operational as from Decemeber when we graduate from the university.We will be located in Mile 17 car station where almost all agencies in Buea are found.
